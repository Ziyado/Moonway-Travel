About Us

Moonway Decorations is based in El Gouna and works across the **entire Red Sea region** including Hurghada, Sahl Hasheesh, Makadi Bay, Soma Bay, Safaga, and Marsa Alam. We specialize in **weddings, parties, corporate events, and seasonal decorations**. With a creative team and trusted partners, we provide complete services from decoration to sound, lighting, logistics, photography, videography, and accommodation arrangements.
